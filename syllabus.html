JEE Syllabus

JEE Main

The JEE Main syllabus includes subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Key topics include:

  • Physics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Electrodynamics, etc.
  • Chemistry: Physical, Organic, and Inorganic Chemistry
  • Mathematics: Algebra, Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, etc.

JEE Advanced

The JEE Advanced syllabus is more challenging and builds on the JEE Main syllabus. It includes advanced topics in the same three subjects.
